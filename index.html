What is Whoami?

In its simplest and shortest definition, Whoami is a user friendly privacy/anonymity tool with its ease of use and ​simple interface. Whoami uses 9+ different modules to ensure the highest possible level of anonymity also solves possible problems without disturbing you with the Bug fixer module, which is in development. 

Finally, don't forget that there is never a hundred percent ​anonymity on the internet!

Keeps your online activity private

Whoami does not track you or does not keep any logs. Hurray zero log policy!

Gives you unrestricted access

Surf the internet freely without being censored or tracked.

Always up to date

If a bug occurs, it will be fixed as soon as possible. No need to worry about your privacy.

Supports multi-distro

Whoami support any Debian or Arch based linux distributions.

It has an user friendly CLI

It helps with auto-complete and history-based suggestion so that even ​beginners can use it without difficulty.
